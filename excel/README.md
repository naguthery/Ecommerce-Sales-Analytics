# Sales Performance Excel File

This folder contains the Excel workbook used for the Sales Performance Analytics project. The workbook was used to clean and transform the data and create PivotTables and KPI calculations for exploratory data analysis.

## Workbook Contents
### Cleaned data worksheet
Contains clened dataset with the addition of revenue, order_month, order_yaer, and age_group coluumns.

### KPI Summary worksheet
Contains calcuulations for total reveenue, total orders, Total Units Sold, Average Order Value, and Average Customer Satisfaction Rating.

### Category worksheet
PivotTable analyzing product category by revenue, average price, and total units sold.

### Products worksheet
PivotTables analyzing top five and bottom five products in terms of revenue. 

### City worksheet
PivotTables analyzing top five and bottom five cities in terms of revenue and number of orders. 

### Age worksheet
PivotTable analyzing revenue by age groups.

### Monthly worksheet
PivotTable analyzing revenue by months and quuarters of 2024 and 2025.

### Payment worksheet
PivotTable analyzing revenue by payment methods.
